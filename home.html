Mission Vision

To provide the citizens of Valenzuela an efficient and
effective institution of higher learning that will make
them skillful, productive, competent, competitive,
civic-minded, God-loving toward a peaceful, healthy,
and progressive City.

A dynamic center for the development of
competent and competitive human resource as
foundation for growth and advancement of the
City of Valenzuela.
